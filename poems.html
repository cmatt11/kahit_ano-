Poems 📝

mga tula na bumaon sa dibdib ko. dramatic? oo. pero totoo.

bakit poems? 🤔

Hindi naman ako yung tipong "oh how the flowers bloom in spring" na tao HAHAHAH. Pero there's something about poems na parang... someone put into words yung feeling na hindi mo ma-explain? Yung tipong binabasa mo tapos bigla mong ma-feel na "AMP, ako 'to."

Most of these nahanap ko nung mga 3am na hindi makatulog, scrolling through random poetry blogs. Others pinasa sakin ng mga kaibigan, or nabasa ko somewhere. Either way, each one tumama sakin differently.

Disclaimer: hindi ako literary critic. I just know what hits me in the gut wahahahha

mga paborito kong tula

in no particular order ha. lahat sila special sakin.

"Still I Rise"

Maya Angelou

Yung energy nito sobrang empowering. Parang sinasabi niya sa mundo "try mo lang, babangon pa rin ako." Pag bad day ko, binabasa ko 'to. Instant serotonin boost. The confidence? Unmatched.

"You may shoot me with your words, you may cut me with your eyes, you may kill me with your hatefulness, but still, like air, I'll rise."

"The Road Not Taken"

Robert Frost

Classic 'to pero for good reason. Every time may decision akong ginagawa na medyo scary, naalala ko 'tong poem na 'to. "I took the one less traveled by" hits different pag nasa crossroads ka talaga sa life. Relate much HWHAHAH.

"Two roads diverged in a wood, and I-- I took the one less traveled by, and that has made all the difference."

"If"

Rudyard Kipling

This poem is basically a survival guide for adulting amp BWAHAHAHA. "If you can keep your head when all about you are losing theirs" -- bro that's literally every sem ng acads. Parang ang galing niya mag-summarize ng "how to not lose your mind."

"If you can meet with Triumph and Disaster and treat those two impostors just the same..."

"Phenomenal Woman"

Maya Angelou

Isa pa 'to ni Maya Angelou kasi sobrang powerful ng words niya. This one's about knowing your worth without needing validation from anyone. Yung tipong you walk into a room and you KNOW. Aspiration ko maging ganito ka-confident tbh. Hwhdhshshahahsha as if daw.

"I'm a woman. Phenomenally. Phenomenal woman, that's me."

"Invictus"

William Ernest Henley

"I am the master of my fate, I am the captain of my soul." GOOSEBUMPS EVERY SINGLE TIME. Sinulat niya 'to while hospitalized and facing amputation. Imagine that level of strength. Pag feeling ko ang hirap ng buhay, I read this and I'm like... okay, kaya ko pa.

"It matters not how strait the gate, how charged with punishments the scroll, I am the master of my fate, I am the captain of my soul."

"Wild Geese"

Mary Oliver

"You do not have to be good. You do not have to walk on your knees for a hundred miles through the desert, repenting." Grabe. Permission to just... exist. Hindi kailangan maging perfect all the time. This poem gave me a hug when I needed it most. 10/10 would cry again AHAHAHA iyakin era.

"Whoever you are, no matter how lonely, the world offers itself to your imagination."
