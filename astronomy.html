Astronomy 🌌

3am, nakatingala sa langit, questioning everything. my favorite hobby.

bakit stars? 🌟

So basically, night owl ako 'di ba? And pag 3am na tapos hindi pa rin makatulog, lumalabas ako for night walks. Tapos naturally, tumingin ka sa taas and... grabe. Yung stars on a clear night? Walang kupas.

There's something humbling about staring at the sky and realizing how MALIIT tayo sa universe. Like, lahat ng problema ko bigla nawala for a moment. Yung assignment na due tomorrow? Walang kwenta compared to the fact that the light I'm seeing traveled millions of years para lang makarating sakin. HAHAHAH okay medyo dramatic pero you get it.

I'm not a legit astronomer or anything ha. Wala akong telescope (SANA MERON). I just... look up. And feel things. That's it.

The Pale Blue Dot

Voyager 1 took a photo of Earth from 6 billion km away. Earth looks like a tiny speck of light. Carl Sagan said "everyone you love, everyone you know, every human being who ever was, lived out their lives on that mote of dust." NA-GOOSEBUMPS KA BA? Hwhdhshshahahsha kasi ako oo.

Stars are dead (sometimes)

Yung mga stars na nakikita natin, some of them wala na. Patay na sila millions of years ago pero yung light nila ngayon lang dumating sa Earth. So technically... ghosts sila. Star ghosts. Poetic 'di ba? Also lowkey creepy.

The Moon

Same moon since the beginning of humanity. Lahat ng tao sa history nakatingala sa same moon na nakikita ko. Cleopatra? Same moon. Rizal? Same moon. My future wife wherever she is right now? SAME MOON. Okay kilig amp wahahahha

Saturn's Rings

Made of ice and rock particles. Some as small as grains of sand, some as big as houses. Also the rings are disappearing slowly. In about 300 million years, wala na sila. So technically we're lucky we exist at a time when they're still visible. Sana makita ko in person someday through a proper telescope huhu.

NASA: Saturn's Rings →
🌊

We're made of star stuff

Hindi 'to exaggeration ha. The carbon in our bodies, the iron in our blood, literally came from stars that exploded billions of years ago. So technically, we ARE the universe experiencing itself. Carl Sagan said that and I think about it every single night. AHAHAHA deep hours nanaman.
