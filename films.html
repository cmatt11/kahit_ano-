movie taste reveal 🍿

I'm the type na pag may pinapanood ako, all in talaga. Like I will THINK about it for days after. Minsan matutulog na ko tapos biglang babalik yung scene sa utak ko and I'm like "GRABE BAKIT GANUN."

the lineup

pag walang mapanood, pick from here. trust me.

Arrival (2016)

THIS MOVIE. Grabe. Hindi siya typical alien movie ha. It's about language, time, love, and choices. Yung ending? Iyak ako nang iyak. Parang... if you knew how things would end, would you still choose it? Sobrang ganda. Watch it please.

Animated pero grownups ang target nito amp. "You become responsible, forever, for what you have tamed." NA-FEEL MO BA YUN? Comfort movie ko 'to forever. Pag nawawala na yung sense of wonder ko sa life, pinapanood ko ulit 'to. AHAHAHA iyakin behavior.

Oo, kasama pa rin 'to despite THAT ending. Seasons 1-6 are peak television and walang makaka-convince sakin otherwise. The writing, the plot twists, the characters... hindi ko ma-explain yung feeling nung unang beses kong napanood Red Wedding. AMP. Hwhdhshshahahsha nawasak ako.

Cartoon pero the humor is for adults honestly BWAHAHAHA. Pag stressed ako, Gumball lang katapat. Walang episode na hindi nakakatawa. Also lowkey may existential themes siya na unexpected for a kids show?? Genius writing.

Comfort show #1. Alam ko na lahat ng episodes pero pinapanood ko pa rin. Something about nerds being nerds is just... relatable? Plus Sheldon is iconic. "Bazinga" lives rent-free in my head. wahahahha puro rewatch.
