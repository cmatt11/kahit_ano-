Games 🎮

pang destress. pang stress din minsan. depende sa araw.

gamer era 🕹️

Hindi naman ako hardcore gamer na may RGB setup at gaming chair (SANA). Pero I play games to escape reality for a bit. Sometimes chill lang, sometimes competitive hanggang magalit na yung kapitbahay sa sigaw ko. HAHAHA

Mix ng relaxing games at "bakit ba ako nag-rank" energy. Walang in between.

Also I game at the worst hours. 2am-5am gaming sessions? Normal. Healthy? Probably not. Worth it? Absolutely.

current rotation

mga nilalaro ko or nilaro ko na na-enjoy ko talaga

🌾

Stardew Valley

Therapy in game form amp. Pag sobrang stressful ng real life, dito ako nagtatago. Mag-aararo, mag-aalaga ng chickens, magdadate ng NPC. Simple life. Bakit hindi ganito ka-peaceful ang totoong buhay? HAHAHA

⚔️

Genshin Impact

Nasimulan ko 'to and hindi ko na alam pano iistop. Yung exploration and lore grabe. Also ang ganda ng music?? Mihoyo/Hoyoverse really said "free game pero AAA quality soundtrack." Walang resin na nasasayang sa exploration naman.

🔫

Valorant

Pang adrenaline rush. Also pang galit HAHAHA. "Diff" "ez" mga toxic teammates. Pero pag nanalo? Best feeling in the world. Iron to Gold arc coming soon manifesting. Jk stuck pa rin. Charot.

⛏️

Minecraft

OG comfort game. Nag-start akong maglaro nito nung bata pa ko and until now bumabalik pa rin ako. Building random stuff at 3am with lo-fi music in the background? That's the life. Creative mode supremacy btw.

🏝️

Animal Crossing

Isa pa 'to sa "escapism in game form." Yung island ko is my dream life na walang bills, walang deadlines, puro fishing at pag-decorate ng bahay. Tom Nook is a capitalist pero at least cute siya. HAHAHA

👻

Phasmophobia

Nilalaro ko 'to with friends tapos sabay-sabay kaming sumasigaw HAHAHA. Horror game siya pero mas nakakatawa kesa nakakatakot pag may kasama ka. "SINO YUNG NASA HALLWAY" "TAKBO" energy. 10/10 bonding experience.
