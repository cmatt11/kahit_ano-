concepts na bumaligtad ng utak ko

mga nareresearch ko at 3am imbes na matulog

🔄

Cognitive Dissonance

Yung feeling na uncomfortable ka kasi hindi nag-align yung beliefs mo sa actions mo. Like, alam mong unhealthy yung ginagawa mo pero ginagawa mo pa rin? That's cognitive dissonance. And we ALL do it. Every. Single. Day. Self-awareness is pain HAHAHA

🧩

Attachment Theory

Grabe 'tong concept na 'to. Basically, yung way na na-attach tayo sa parents natin nung bata pa tayo, it affects how we love people as adults. Anxious attachment? Avoidant? Disorganized? Na-explain niya lahat ng past relationships ko. HAHAHA ay charot. Pero seriously, game-changer 'to in understanding yourself.

📈

Dunning-Kruger Effect

People who know very little about something tend to overestimate their ability. While experts tend to underestimate theirs. Kaya pala ang daming confident na mali. And yung mga matalino feeling nila bobo sila. RELATABLE. Also this explains a LOT about people on the internet HAHAHA

🪞

The Spotlight Effect

We think people notice us more than they actually do. Yung tipong na-trip ka sa hallway and you think everyone saw? They didn't. Promise. Everyone's too busy thinking about themselves. Liberating 'di ba? You're not the main character in other people's lives. And that's OKAY.

🎭

Projection

When people accuse you of something they're actually guilty of. "You're so selfish!" says the person na never nag-compromise. Once you learn about projection, you'll see it EVERYWHERE. Also sobrang useful for understanding toxic people without losing your mind.

🫧

Confirmation Bias

We tend to look for information that confirms what we already believe. And ignore everything else. Kaya ang hirap mag-change ng mind ng tao. Also kaya ang toxic ng social media echo chambers. Once you're aware of it, you start catching yourself doing it too. Uncomfortable pero necessary.

🧸

Inner Child Wounds

Yung mga unresolved feelings from childhood na nagma-manifest sa adult behavior natin. Yung takot sa rejection? Yung need for validation? Yung difficulty setting boundaries? Usually galing sa experiences nung bata ka. Masakit i-confront pero understanding it is the first step to healing. Deep 'to ha HAHAHA

⏰

Zeigarnik Effect

Incomplete tasks stay in your memory more than completed ones. KAYA PALA hindi ako maka-move on sa mga conversations na hindi natapos nang maayos?? Also explains why cliffhangers work so well. Our brain hates loose ends. It all makes sense now amp.
